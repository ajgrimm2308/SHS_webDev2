Batarang

The Bat is a lightweight traversal tool in the unique shape of a bat. You can either use both hands to slide along pipes, or use one hand to transfer between pipes. Both the bat-shaped design and black grit texture provide a comfortable and secure grip.

Boltcutters

The Bolt Cutters were designed to challenge the upper body strength and grip of elite ninjas. This obstacle requires you to open and close the jaws of the Bolt Cutters to latch onto various solid structures, such as pipes, skyhooks, and rings.
