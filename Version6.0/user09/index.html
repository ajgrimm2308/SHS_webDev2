About:

I'm Ewan Kahnke and this is Salmon Says. I got the idea to do this from my grandpa. It was August of 2023 and I was in Florida. Salmon has always been one of my favorite foods so after my third salmon meal in as many days, my grandpa suggested that I should start reviewing all the different salmon dishes I have. So I did. And here we are. My mom came up with the name after my grandpa introduced the idea and I immediately loved it. So sit back and enjoy seeing what Salmon Says.
