Date:

8/3/23 - Ichabod's Dockside

Description:

Salmon Caprese Sandwich

With fresh mozzarella, basil, tomato, and balsamic reduction with a side of steamed broccoli.

Everything was so fresh. The original side was chips but I subbed it for the broccoli and it went perfect with the sandwich. The giant piece of mozzarella was great. There was slightly too much basil, it being the main taste in too many bites. The sauce was a little sweeter than I would have liked but it was still good. The salmon was almost perfect, just the slightest but too dry. The atmosphere of the resturant was amazing. Overall, very good experience.

Review: 8/10
