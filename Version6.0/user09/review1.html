Description:

Citrus Salmon

The salmon was perfect. Slightly juicy just how I like it. The citrus sauce on top was overwhelmingly citrusy. Felt like biting into a lemon. Scraped most of it off and it was fine. Sides were garlic mashed potatoes and mixed veggies. They were both good but the pototoes were excellent. The sauce is the only think preventing this from being a perfect rating.

Review: 9/10
