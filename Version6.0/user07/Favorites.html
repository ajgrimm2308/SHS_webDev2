Games

Subnautica

Summary:

Subnautica is an underwater open-world survival game. You play as a crew member on the Aroura, which ends up crash-landing on an alien planet. Collect resources to survive and venture deeper do discover the secrets of this planet.
My review: Very good, will give you thalassaphobia. 9.5/10

Minecraft

Summary:

Minecraft is an open-world 3d survival game with practically no limits. Every world is randomly generated and everything is cubic. There are no rules or limits other than your imagination.
My review: One of my top favorite games of all time. It brings me so many good memories. 9.9/10

OneShot

Summary:

OneShot is a story-driven 2d puzzle game. You follow Niko, a small child who awoke in a strange world, destined to return the light to it.
My review: An incredibly underrated game and the solutions to the puzzles are really creative. Apparently it's also different the second playthrough. 9/10

Ghostrunner

Summary:

Ghostrunner is a fast-paced 3d platforming game where you play as a... well... Ghostrunner, making your way through levels and killing enemies to get your abilities back and take down the opressive leader, with your AI companion, The Architect.
My review: You end up dying a lot in this game, which can get frustrating, but it can get really satisfying at times. 8/10

VRChat

Summary:

VRChat is an online VR chatroom game. It's a very community based game that houses over 25,000 worlds and millions of avatars to choose from. My review: There's so many things to do in this game it's honestly crazy. From magnificent scenery to incredibly complex and fun games. This game is 100% worth the try. 10/10

Beat Saber

Summary:

Beat Saber is a Virtual Reality rythm game where you have to slice incoming "bloqs" in the direction of the arrows.
My review: This is a relatively small game, but once you add mods to the mix, that's when things get interesting, with thousands of songs to choose from, some really well designed and beautiful. When charts are made well, it's really fun. 8.5/10
