My Favorite Music

5 Favorite Albums of All Time:

Rank Artist Album Name & Explanation Album cover
1 Tame Impala Currents - This is my favorite album of all time. It has a depth and sound to it unlike any other album Kevin Parker, the genius behind Tame Impala, has ever made. It sounds like you're going on an epic space odyssey.
2 Tame Impala Innerspeaker - Innerspeaker is Tame Impala's debut studio album. This album has a 60's psychedelic rock twist that is addictive to say the least.
3 Psychedelic Porn Crumpets SHYGA! The Sunlight Mound - A mix of sprawling, exploratory, psych-pop, prog rock, and heavy garage riffs, Australia's Psychedelic Porn Crumpets rose out of Perth's indie underground.
4 Nirvana Nevermind - Though the album is considered a cornerstone of the grunge genre, it is noted for its musical diversity. It has ballads as well as some harder punk rock classics.
5 Pi'erre Bourne The Life of Pi'erre 5 - The Life of Pi'erre 5 is a colorful experience that personifies the aesthetic of the cult icon. His retro video-game inspired beat production, and seamless trasitions into each song makes listening a delightful experience
