My favorite band is a band called beartooth. They are an all around great band that has very little bad songs.
1 Beartooth Beartooth is an American rock band founded by vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Caleb Shomo in Columbus, Ohio, in 2012. Originally Shomo performed every instrument when in the studio, before being joined by a four-piece band. Beartooth has been signed to Red Bull Records since 2013
If you want a band that is a lot harder and has a lot heavier sound, there is a band that can do that. The band that I like that does that is called Slaughter to Prevail.
2 Slaughter to Prevail Slaughter to Prevail is a Russian deathcore band originally from Yekaterinburg, and based in Orlando, Florida. The band is currently signed to Sumerian Records and have toured through the US, Europe, Canada, and Asia.
If you like it softer though, you can check out my previous favorite band, Shinedown.
3 Shinedown Shinedown is an American rock band from Jacksonville, Florida, formed by singer Brent Smith in 2001 after the dissolution of his previous band, Dreve. Smith, still under contract with record label Atlantic Records.
