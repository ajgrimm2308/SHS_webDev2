My favorite Manga is One Piece. It is a great story that has very little wrong with it. It has good depth, world-building, and action that make it just a very enjoyable experience to read.
1 One Piece The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, where he explores the Grand Line in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.
My second favorite manga is based in history and is a lot more emotional than One Piece. It is very deep and has a very strong message that is told throughout the story. It is called Vinland Saga.
2 Vinland Saga The story is a dramatization of the story of Thorfinn Karlsefni and his expedition to find Vinland, with the majority of the story covering his fictional counterpart's transition from a bloodthirsty, revenge-filled teenager into a pacifistic young man; juxtaposed against this is the rise to power of King Canute, the journey of his own counterpart directly contrasting with that of Thorfinn's.
Both of those manga are very popular though. If you want something that is more underground but still very good. It is the manga, Toriko.
3 Toriko It follows the adventures of Toriko, a Gourmet Hunter, as he searches for rare, diverse foods to complete a full-course meal. On his journey, he is accompanied by a timid chef who wants to improve his skills.
